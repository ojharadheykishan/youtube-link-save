✅ Custom Video Player Test

🎮 Player Controls

  • Speed: Click speed button (1x) - Try 0.25x, 0.5x, 1x, 2x, 4x
  • Quality: Click quality button (Auto) - Try 4K, 1080p, 720p, 480p
  • Play/Pause: Click play button or press SPACE
  • Fullscreen: Click fullscreen button or press F
  • Mute: Click volume button or press M
  • Seek: Click on progress bar or use Arrow keys (±5s)
  • Volume: Use ↑/↓ arrows to adjust volume

📋 Test Checklist

  • ❌ Player initialized
  • ❌ Controls visible
  • ❌ Speed control working
  • ❌ Play/pause working
  • ❌ Progress bar working
